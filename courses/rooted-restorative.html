Rooted & Restorative

A gentle video course for releasing tension and rebuilding mobility — slow, therapeutic sessions designed for beginners and anyone returning to practice.

About this course

Slow down. Release. Restore.

Each lesson moves at an unhurried pace — grounding breathwork, supported postures, and therapeutic sequences that respect where your body is today. Every session runs at least 30 minutes and stands on its own.

Curriculum

6 recorded lessons

  1. Lesson 01

    Grounding & Breath

    Diaphragmatic breathing, body scan, and simple seated movements to arrive on the mat with ease.

    30 min

    35 min

    Release Tension

    40 min

    Supported Restorative

    Bolster and block-supported poses held with breath — deep rest for the nervous system.

    45 min
  5. Lesson 05

    Slow Flow Building

    Linking gentle movements with breath — a bridge between stillness and flowing practice.

    40 min
  6. Lesson 06

    Full Restorative Session

    The complete course practice — grounding, release, supported rest, and a long, quiet savasana.

    50 min
