Curriculum

10 lessons · 45 minutes each

7.5 hours of guided practice

  1. Lesson 01

    Arrive & Soften

    45 min

    Grounding breath, gentle joint circles, and the art of arriving on the mat without force.

  2. Lesson 02

    Neck & Shoulders

    45 min

    Release the upper body — desk tension, phone posture, and the weight we carry in the shoulders.

  3. Lesson 03

    Hip Opening Basics

    45 min

    Supported pigeon, figure-four, and gentle hip flexor work for sitting-tight hips.

  4. Lesson 04

    Spine Mobility

    45 min

    Cat-cow variations, gentle twists, and segmental movement to restore a fluid spine.

  5. Lesson 05

    Hamstrings & Back Body

    45 min

    Forward folds with props and patience — length without strain, breath without rush.

  6. Lesson 06

    Gentle Twists

    45 min

    Seated and supine twists that wring out tension and invite space around the organs and ribs.

  7. Lesson 07

    Supported Backbends

    45 min

    Bridge and gentle heart openers with support — open the chest without compressing the low back.

  8. Lesson 08

    Nervous System Reset

    45 min

    Longer holds, yoga nidra elements, and breath that signals safety to the body.

  9. Lesson 09

    Full-Body Restore

    45 min

    A complete restorative sequence linking hips, spine, and breath into one soft arc.

  10. Lesson 10

    Deep Rest Practice

    45 min

    The finale — your go-to 45-minute reset for any week that asks too much of you.
