Curriculum

10 lessons · 45 minutes each

7.5 hours of guided practice

  1. Lesson 01

    Breath & Arrival

    45 min

    Ujjayi breath, joint warm-up, and the foundations of vinyasa — how inhale and exhale guide every transition.

  2. Lesson 02

    Sun Salutation A

    45 min

    Surya Namaskar A broken down pose by pose, then woven into a steady, breath-led rhythm.

  3. Lesson 03

    Sun Salutation B

    45 min

    Warrior I, chair, and the longer salutation — building heat while keeping the breath smooth.

  4. Lesson 04

    Standing Foundations

    45 min

    Warrior II, triangle, and side angle — strength through the legs, length through the side body.

  5. Lesson 05

    Balance & Focus

    45 min

    Tree, eagle, and standing balances that train the gaze, the core, and quiet concentration.

  6. Lesson 06

    Hips & Twists

    45 min

    Pigeon, lizard, and seated twists to release the hips and restore mobility through the spine.

  7. Lesson 07

    Core Intelligence

    45 min

    Navasana, plank variations, and deep abdominal work linked to breath — stability before power.

  8. Lesson 08

    Heart Openers

    45 min

    Cobra, locust, and bridge — safe entry into backbending with shoulder and spinal preparation.

  9. Lesson 09

    Flow Integration

    45 min

    Everything linked: salutations, standing work, and floor sequence into one continuous mid-length flow.

  10. Lesson 10

    The Full Practice

    45 min

    The course finale — a complete vinyasa from opening breath to savasana. Your practice, owned.
