About

Engineering precision. Yoga presence.

My practice began far from the mat — in medical device engineering, where structure, detail, and care are non-negotiable. That discipline now defines how I teach: alignment that honors anatomy, breath that sets the pace, and sequences built with purpose.

As an RYT 300 certified instructor, I guide students of every level toward strength, mobility, and mental clarity — in the studio, by the sea, or wherever practice finds you.
