About Nora

An engineer's precision. A yogi's stillness.

My path to yoga started far from the mat — in the exacting world of medical device engineering, where every detail matters and nothing is left to chance. That same discipline now shapes how I teach: alignment that respects the body's structure, breath that paces every movement, and sequences built with intention rather than trend.

RYT 300 certified, I've spent the past years guiding students of every level toward strength, mobility, and a quieter mind — on the mat, by the sea, or wherever stillness finds them.
